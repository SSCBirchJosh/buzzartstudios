Can I be Tattooed Anywhere?

Technically, you can be tattooed ANYWHERE. Your consultation with one of our designers/artists will determine where you can have a tattoo as we bring it down to the designers/artists preference.

How Long Will My Tattoo Take?

The length of time will depend on the size, amount of colour, detail and your pain threshhold. Your artist will outline a rough time for your tattoo to be complete.

How Will I Decide On What I Want?

You can book a consultation with one of our designers/artists through the "Tattoo Bookings" tab in the menu.

Can I Use Numbing Cream or Painkillers?

Using numbing cream is not recommended as it needs to be applied 30 minutes before your tattoo. If you are going to use numbing cream, apply it before you come into the studio. In terms of painkillers, aspirin is not recommended as it thins the blood and reduces clotting, this will result in excessive bleeding during your tattoo. Regular over the counter paracetomol can be used but won't have a major effect, mainly a placebo.
